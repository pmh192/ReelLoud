The Festival is Open to:

  • Current UCSB students
  • UCSB Alumni
  • Santa Barbara County Community Members

Submissions are open to:

  • Open: Monday, April 1st, 2019, 12:00pm
  • Submission Deadline: Friday, April 26th, 2019, 5:00pm
  • Final Deadline for Selected Films: TBA

Competing films must be:

  • No more than 7 minutes in length, credits included.
  • Not created or any way produced by members of the REEL LOUD organizational committee.
    • Exceptions can be made to this rule at the discretion of the committee.If the committee member(s) in question agrees to remove themselves from the submission selection process, the film is eligible for competition.

Preliminary Submissions for Selection:

  • Include temporary soundtrack and sound effects on your preliminary submission that is an accurate estimate of what will played live during the night of the festival.
    • Submissions submitted without a temporary musical score are less likely to be selected.
    • You must have all live performers locked at the time of preliminary submission. Films without a performance group at this time will not be eligible for selection.
  • Submit to:
    • FAMST Main Office - SSMS 2433
      • Hard Drive, Flash Drive, Blu Ray, or DVD
      • Your hardcopy vehicle will be returned.
    • Email: reelloudfilm@gmail.com
      • Link to uploaded video
      • Make the video private or password protected if possible
      • Subject line “Preliminary Submission”
  • We select films for the festival based only on what is submitted. Please note the following:
    • A final cut is highly recommended but not required.
    • Double check that your film is viewable via the link or hardware that you submit it on.
    • Incomplete films are much less likely to be selected than finished products.

Final submissions must be:

  • Completely silent
  • Exported on settings for Vimeo 1920 x 1080
  • Leave 3 seconds of black before and after your film for transition purposes
  • Hard Copy to the FAMST Main Office - SSMS 2433

Musical Accompaniment:

  • Your film will be accompanied the night of the festival by live music from a musician of your choosing.
    • No musical accompaniment group may exceed 8 members.
  • You are allowed to use licensed music as it will be performed live in an educational setting and it is thus fair use.
  • No completely pre-recorded tracks are allowed.
  • Submitting to the festival implies the acceptance that all your musicians will attend their rehearsal time the evening before the festival as well as the entirety of the festival day.
    • Failure of your band to attend rehearsal can lead to your film being removed from the line up.

Performance accompaniment:

  • You are allowed to have a live performance aspect of your film presentation as long as it fits within the 7 minute runtime.
  • You must describe the live performance aspect in detail on the submission form during the preliminary submission.
    • This includes any and all props that you will be bringing on stage.
  • Any live performance aspect is subject to review and approval by Campbell Hall Staff.
    • If your live act is deemed unsafe by a fire marshall, we reserve the right to remove it from the line up.

Content:

  • There are no restrictions on the content of your films.
  • Films may use stock/ found footage
    • The footage must be altered or edited in some way to show original creative thought.
    • Films using any pre-existing footage will not be considered for the category of “Best Cinematography.”
  • Please note that the festival is open audiences of all ages.
  • We reserve the right to place a disclaimer/ trigger warning before your film to alert the audience.

Rights:

  • All rights to the film are maintained by the filmmaker.
  • The festival claims no ownership or responsibility for the production of final films.
  • The festival reserves the right to use all submitted content for advertising and other promotional efforts for the current and future events.

Art Submission Period:

  • Submission Deadline: Friday, April 26th, 2019, 5:00pm
  • Open: Monday, April 1st, 2019, 12:00pm

Art:

  • Art submissions are ideally in conjunction with the festival theme.
  • Submit photos of entry to reelloudfilm@gmail.com with the following information:
    • Title of Piece
    • Artist Name
    • Medium type
    • Dimensions [if applicable]
  • All artists must pick up their art the night of the festival at the conclusion of the courtyard reception.
  • The festival will supply easels and video monitors as best they can as budget permits.
    • Maximum 5 video monitors

Other important points:

  • The committee will select films based on a variety of factors including but not limited to time, aesthetics, themes, spirit, vibe, technical considerations, and logistics of live performances. All this is to say that there are many factors that go into programing a festival and whether your film is selected or not does not necessarily reflect your skill as a filmmaker.
    • The REEL LOUD Committee reserves total control over selection of the films and the decisions of the selection committee are final.
  • Individual filmmakers are welcome to promote their individual films across campus.
    • We encourage submitting a poster with your film so that the Reel Loud Publicity Team can promote your film on our social media platforms leading up to the festival.
  • Filmmakers are allowed submit more than one film.
    • This does not increase the filmmaker’s chances of being chosen for the festival. We do not recommend linking the plots of multiple submissions.
  • All performers musical or otherwise must sign a performance contract in order to appear at the festival.
  • If a competitor has any questions, comments, concerns or oh-my-gosh’s, please send us an email at reelloudfilm@gmail.com or use our contact page.
